# Open Food Facts Products Snapshot

Pinned Open Food Facts search response with product barcode and completeness metrics.

Pinned source: `https://world.openfoodfacts.org/cgi/search.pl?search_terms=chocolate&search_simple=1&action=process&json=1&page_size=100`

Selected series:
- `openfoodfacts_code`
- `openfoodfacts_additives_n`
- `openfoodfacts_complete`
- `openfoodfacts_completeness`
- `openfoodfacts_unique_scans_n`

Missing-value policy: Filters out products missing barcode or selected count fields.
